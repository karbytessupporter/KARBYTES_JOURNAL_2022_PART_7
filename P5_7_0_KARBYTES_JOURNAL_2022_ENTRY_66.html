For the first time in my life, I went to Zide Door in Oakland (which is within walking distance of Fruitvale BART station) to purchase psilocybin (i.e. magic mushrooms) on three different occasions this summer. In June, I purchased approximately 7 grams of “ghost penis envy” (which is allegedly one of the strongest strains of psilocybin) for approximately $100. I did it all in one sitting and had some profound epistemological insights and weird dreamlike revelations. I plan to do another “heroic dose” of that stuff later this year and to write a resulting trip report which I deem to be worthy of being included in the Production Quality Journals of the KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022 section of this website (and the Production Quality Journals represent what the author of this website thinks are higher quality journal entries than the web pages within the KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022 section of this website which are classified as Warmup Journals).
